CodeBrix.Platform.Fonts.Fluent is a CodeBrix-family redistribution of the
Uno Platform Fluent icon font, packaged as a .NET 10 NuGet library. The
package incorporates third-party material from a single upstream source:

     shape (the `.props` MSBuild logic and the `.uprimarker` mechanism)
